# GreenScape Modern Theme

A fresh, vibrant theme designed specifically for GreenScape Lawn Care, combining nature-inspired colors with modern design aesthetics.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Emerald Green** - `#10b981` (Primary brand color - vibrant, healthy lawn green)
- **Deep Forest** - `#065f46` (Dark accent - professional, trustworthy)
- **Mint Fresh** - `#d1fae5` (Light accent - fresh, clean)

### Secondary Colors
- **Sky Blue** - `#0ea5e9` (Trust, reliability)
- **Sunshine Yellow** - `#fbbf24` (Energy, optimism)
- **Earth Brown** - `#78350f` (Grounding, natural)

### Neutral Colors
- **Slate Dark** - `#1e293b` (Headings, text)
- **Slate Medium** - `#475569` (Body text)
- **Slate Light** - `#f1f5f9` (Backgrounds)
- **White** - `#ffffff` (Clean backgrounds)

### Gradient Colors
- **Hero Gradient** - `linear-gradient(135deg, #10b981 0%, #059669 50%, #065f46 100%)`
- **Card Gradient** - `linear-gradient(to bottom right, #ffffff 0%, #f0fdf4 100%)`
- **Accent Gradient** - `linear-gradient(90deg, #0ea5e9 0%, #10b981 100%)`

## Typography

### Headers
- **Font Family**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Weight: 700-800)
- Modern, clean, highly readable
- Excellent for digital displays

### Body Text
- **Font Family**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Weight: 400-500)
- Clean and professional
- Great readability at all sizes

### Accent Text
- **Font Family**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Weight: 600)
- Used for CTAs and emphasis
